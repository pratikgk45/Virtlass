Services Available


For Every User

  1. Each user need to sign up to enter the portal for the first time.
  2. User need to provide their required information while signing up. They can also add profile picture as well. They can update this information any time they want.
  3. User can login into the portal and do their required activity.

For Teacher

  1. Teacher can add student in their class through Add Student option.
  2. Teacher can upload assignments/tests through upload assignment options.
  3. Teacher can check their student's assignment submission and give them marks accordingly. They can update it as well if needed.
  4. Teacher can also write notice to all students in their class.

For Student

  1. Students can login in the portal and keep themselves updated of all the activities going on in the class.
  2. Students can download their assignments in the Assignments option and can also submit it (Once submitted, it can't be changed later).
  3. Students can see the notice given by their teacher in the Class Notice option.
  4. Students can contact any teacher by messaging them.
